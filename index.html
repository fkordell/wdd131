South Africa

South Africa is a country rich with diversity, known for its stunning landscapes and a melting pot of cultures. It is distinguished by its several ecosystems, from the beautiful beaches of the Western Cape, the lush winelands, to the dramatic cliffs at the Cape of Good Hope, and the dense bush of the Kruger National Park, which is populated with big game. The country has 11 official languages, which is a testament to its cultural diversity.
